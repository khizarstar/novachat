PRIVACY POLICY
Last Updated: July 1, 2026

📋 1. Data We Collect & Scope

To deliver advanced AI assistance and reliable community features, Nova Chat processes essential data objects. This is strictly limited to:

  • Discord Identifiers: User IDs, Server (Guild) IDs, and Channel IDs.
  • Message Metadata: Temporarily checked solely to execute features like conversational responses and AI context.
  • Configuration State: Custom settings designed by server staff for bot behavior.

What We Do Not Collect: We never collect, cache, or ask for clear-text personal credentials, emails, authentication passwords, or financial transaction details.

📊 2. How We Utilize Your Data

The operational data processed by the bot backend is reserved exclusively to fuel functionality components. We process information to:

  • Provide accurate AI-driven responses to your queries.
  • Maintain the operational integrity of the bot within your server.
  • Troubleshoot technical faults and mitigate backend bugs.

Nova Chat maintains a strict zero-monetization policy over user data. We do not sell, rent, license, or trade your server context or individual data to third-party companies.

🛡️ 3. Security Architecture & Storage

All configuration layouts and user context are held securely using industry-standard database structures. We apply standard security measures to protect sensitive data elements.

⏳ 4. Data Retention & Automatic Purging

If Nova Chat leaves a Discord server, all localized server logs and configuration setups are automatically earmarked for deletion within 30 days.

📧 5. Data Deletion & Privacy Rights

Server owners and individual users maintain full authority over their stored records. If you wish to manually initiate a wipe of your data, please transmit your official request along with your respective Discord snowflake IDs to: contact.novachat.bot@gmail.com. Requests are typically audited and processed within 7 business days.
