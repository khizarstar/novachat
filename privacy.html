PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy explicitly details what persistent information Nova Chat collects from your Discord guild ecosystem, how that specific data is maintained, and the clear rights you hold as a guild administrator or user.

1. Data Collection

Nova Chat collects only the essential data required to provide its services to your Discord server. This includes:

  • Discord IDs of users interacting with the bot.
  • Server IDs where the bot is implemented.
  • The text of messages processed when the AI is triggered (to generate appropriate responses).

2. Data Usage

The information collected is used solely to:

  • Maintain the functionality of the bot’s features.
  • Ensure the security and operational integrity of the service.
  • Respond to user inquiries or commands.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Your information is kept confidential and is used strictly for the operation of Nova Chat within your guild.

4. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to provide our services. You may request the deletion of your server's data at any time by contacting our support team.

5. Third-Party Services

Nova Chat may interact with external APIs to generate content. We ensure that our usage of such services complies with their respective privacy standards and our commitment to your data security.

6. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Continued use of Nova Chat constitutes your agreement to the updated terms.
