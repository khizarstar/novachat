COMMUNITY GUIDELINES

🛡️ Respect All Members

The cornerstone of our community is the mutual respect held between all members. We enforce a strictly professional environment where harassment, bullying, or hate speech is met with immediate intervention.

💬 No Spamming or Flooding

Operational clarity requires a clean interface. Spamming, excessive emoji usage, or mass mentions will be neutralized by our automated filters.

🔗 Strict Anti-Advertising Policy

The unauthorized promotion of third-party servers, commercial services, or external invite links is strictly prohibited and will lead to permanent restriction.

🧭 Proper Channel Utilization

Every channel serves a distinct purpose. Please ensure your contributions align with the channel's intent to maintain an orderly environment.

🚫 Zero Tolerance for Inappropriate Content

Content deemed NSFW, explicit, or graphically violent is strictly forbidden. Safety is our non-negotiable operational standard.

⚠️ Warning: Using Nova Chat for inappropriate purposes or questions may result in a permanent ban.
